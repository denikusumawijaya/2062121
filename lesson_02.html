Spiral Review Warm-up

Basic geometry and angle relationships

Parallel Lines

Two lines extending in the same direction without ever meeting.

Dua garis yang memanjang ke arah yang sama tanpa pernah bertemu (sejajar).

Transversal Line

A line that crosses two parallel lines, creating special angle pairs (Corresponding, Alternate, Co-interior).

Garis yang memotong dua garis sejajar, membentuk pasangan sudut khusus (Sehadap, Berseberangan, Sepihak).

Angle Sums

Supplementary: Adds to \( 180^\circ \) (Straight line). Complementary: Adds to \( 90^\circ \) (Corner).

Berpelurus (jumlahnya 180°) dan Berpenyiku (jumlahnya 90°).

Real-World Connection

Where math meets architecture

Structural Integrity

Architecture relies on parallel lines and transversals. In cable-stayed bridges, cables must align perfectly to distribute weight evenly.

Arsitektur bergantung pada garis sejajar dan transversal. Kabel jembatan harus sejajar sempurna agar beban merata.

Skyscrapers & Design

Parallel steel beams support level floors. Transversal beams cross them, creating predictable angle patterns for engineers.

Balok baja sejajar menopang lantai rata. Balok transversal melintanginya, menciptakan pola sudut yang bisa diprediksi.

Interior Aesthetics

Even floor tiles use parallel lines. Without precise angle measurements, patterns would appear crooked and unprofessional.

Ubin lantai pun menggunakan garis sejajar. Tanpa sudut yang tepat, polanya akan terlihat miring dan tidak profesional.

Prior Knowledge

The Z, F, and C Patterns

'Z' & 'F' Patterns (Equal)

The 'Z' pattern represents alternate interior angles, and 'F' highlights corresponding angles. Both are ALWAYS equal.

Pola 'Z' = sudut dalam berseberangan. Pola 'F' = sudut sehadap. Keduanya selalu SAMA BESAR.

'C' Pattern (Supplementary)

The 'C' pattern shows co-interior angles. Unlike Z and F, they are NOT equal; they are supplementary (add to \( 180^\circ \)).

Pola 'C' = sudut dalam sepihak. Tidak sama besar, melainkan berpelurus (jumlahnya \( 180^\circ \)).

Problem Introduction

Combining algebra and geometry

Angles as Variables

Angles aren't always simple numbers; they can be algebraic expressions like \( 3x + 10 \).

Sudut tidak selalu angka sederhana; bisa berupa ekspresi aljabar seperti \( 3x + 10 \).

Creating Equations

If two angles are corresponding, equate their expressions: \( 3x + 10 = 2x + 40 \). Solve for \( x \)!

Jika dua sudut sehadap, samakan ekspresinya: \( 3x + 10 = 2x + 40 \). Selesaikan untuk mencari \( x \)!

ALGEBRAIC STEPS

  • 1 Identify relationship (e.g., Corresponding = Equal)
  • 2 Set up equation: \( 3x + 10 = 2x + 40 \)
  • 3 Isolate \( x \): \( x = 30 \)
  • 4 Substitute back: \( 3(30) + 10 = 100^\circ \)

Solving co-interior angle equations

Co-Interior Sum: \( 180^\circ \)

Co-interior angles always sum to \( 180^\circ \). If angles are \( 5x \) and \( x + 30 \), the equation is \( 5x + x + 30 = 180 \).

Sudut dalam sepihak selalu berjumlah \( 180^\circ \). Jika sudutnya \( 5x \) dan \( x + 30 \), persamaannya \( 5x + x + 30 = 180 \).

Solving & Verifying

Combine terms: \( 6x + 30 = 180 \) → \( 6x = 150 \) → \( x = 25 \). Always verify if the sum equals 180!

Gabungkan suku: \( 6x + 30 = 180 \) → \( 6x = 150 \) → \( x = 25 \). Selalu verifikasi bahwa jumlahnya 180!

Multi-Line Problems

Complex figures may have multiple parallel lines cut by more than one transversal. Break them into simpler parts!

Gambar kompleks bisa punya banyak garis sejajar dan transversal. Pecah menjadi bagian yang lebih sederhana!

Transitive Property

If line \( a \parallel b \) and \( b \parallel c \), then \( a \parallel c \). This is vital for multi-layered architectural designs.

Jika garis \( a \parallel b \) dan \( b \parallel c \), maka \( a \parallel c \). Sangat penting untuk desain arsitektur berlapis.
